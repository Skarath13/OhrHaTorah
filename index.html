About Us

Congregation Ohr HaTorah is an independent, Messianic synagogue located in the heart of Orange County, California. We warmly welcome all Jewish, non-Jewish, and interfaith couples and families, all who desire to experience and grow in the deep richness of Jewish life, thought, and tradition.

Our community and services are ideal both for those who are inquiring and for those who seek to deepen their understanding of the Jewish Messiah, Yeshua.

Ours is a home where the teachings of Torah and the teachings of Yeshua come together as one, where we as life-long learners of the Bible and of Judaism can grow together with love, respect, and joy.
