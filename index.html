Welcome to Ohr HaTorah Messianic Congregation

Shabbat Service This Week: Join us Saturday at 10:00 AM for worship, Torah reading (Parasha Kedoshim), and fellowship meal at our Tustin location.

Ohr HaTorah ("Light of the Torah") is Orange County's vibrant congregation of Jewish and non-Jewish believers in Yeshua as the Messiah of Israel. We observe the biblical Sabbath and festivals while embracing the completed work of Messiah.

Our congregation honors the Torah and celebrates the Jewish roots of our faith while recognizing Yeshua as the fulfillment of the Messianic prophecies. We welcome all who seek to explore the Hebrew roots of faith!

Rabbi's Message

Shalom friends! As we approach Passover, we remember the deliverance of our people from Egypt and the greater deliverance we have through our Messiah Yeshua. Join us for our community Seder as we celebrate this special time together in our beautiful Orange County facility.

Finding Ohr HaTorah has been a blessing for our family. The blend of Jewish traditions and faith in Yeshua has enriched our spiritual journey in ways we never expected.

Rachel Cohen Congregation Member since 2020
