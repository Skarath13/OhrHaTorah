Our Community

At Ohr HaTorah, we are a diverse and welcoming community united in our love for God and dedication to Jewish tradition and faith in Yeshua. Our congregation includes:

  • Jewish believers in Yeshua
  • Interfaith families
  • Seekers and learners
  • People of all backgrounds

Leadership
